3 Fun facts about Camels

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Don’t make a camel angry – they can spit as a way to distract whatever they think is a threat!
  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go!

3 Fun facts about Pandas

  • Only about 1,500 of these black-and-white relatives of bears survive in the wild. 
  • Pandas eat fast, they eat a lot, and they spend about 12 hours a day doing it.
  • An adult female panda weighs 200 pounds.

3 Fun facts about Flying Cicadas

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • Cicadas are the only insects capable of producing a unique and loud sound.

3 Fun facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleon eyes have a 360-degree arc of vision and can see two directions at once.
  • These colorful lizards are known as one of the few animals that can change skin color.
  • Chameleons don't change color to match their surroundings, it is a way to maintain their body temperature!
